Welcome to

Language + Culture
Exchange Club

LCE encourages students who are interested in learning languages of all kinds and travel to join together in a safe, inclusive group where we all support each other's language-learning journey.

Join today!

Study Groups

Join groups of students learning the same language as you are. All languages are welcome! Study and practice together with new friends in an encouraging, judgement-free community of other language-learners. Learning multiple languages? Feel free to join different language groups during each club meeting to practice a different language each week!
